This is an archive of guitar fingerstyle videos and their corresponding fingerstyle tabs. I created this archive due to the lack of tablature and song connection on the internet. Lots of youtube fingerstylists make beautiful arrangements that get millions of views and exposure, and people really want to play these songs. The issue though, is that not everyone is good at the art of fingerstyle tabbing, or doesn't have good ear, but would still like to play these songs. This archive remedies that in the sense where everything you need to play the song is here for you. People seem to think that playing these fingerstyle arrangements is daunting and impossible, people just want to quit guitar when they see someone pay soething amazing. I get inspired by the challenge, and I think that when people see the tab next to the song that they will not be as afraid, and I encourage you all to attempt learning what you want to learn.
